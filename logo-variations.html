Ebrostay — logo variations

Ten minimalist takes on the mark, all built from Zaragoza's Puente de Piedra — its row of stone arches over the Ebro and the four lion columns at its ends — with several pairing it with the Basílica del Pilar (the classic postcard view), plus one nod to Calatrava's Puente del Tercer Milenio. Each is shown at icon sizes, in the header lockup, and on the dark footer. Every file is a drop-in replacement for assets/ebrostay-icon.svg.
