For international HR / global-mobility contacts (multinationals with a Zaragoza
site). Replace the `{{merge fields}}` before sending and keep the
legitimate-interest + opt-out footer on **every** email (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).

Merge fields: `{{company}}`, `{{name}}` (contact first name or "HR team"),
`{{sender}}`, `{{sender_role}}`, `{{phone}}`, `{{web}}` (ebrostay.com),
`{{unsubscribe}}` (unsubscribe link or "reply STOP to opt out").
